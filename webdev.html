Project Overview

Stelli is an open-source Django web application designed to streamline dormitory management by providing an intuitive and efficient platform for students to organize bookings, share information, and collaborate within their community.

I built Stelli to simplify the management of shared spaces in student dorms, allowing tenants to book exercise rooms, music rooms, and study rooms seamlessly. The platform includes an advanced calendar view, real-time availability tracking, and booking restrictions to ensure fair usage of shared resources.
